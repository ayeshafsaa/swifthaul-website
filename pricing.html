FAQ

Pricing Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and billing.

Is there a setup or onboarding fee?

No — there are zero setup fees on any plan. You pay only the monthly plan price starting from your first active month. Onboarding is completely free.

Can I switch plans later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle with no penalties or fees.

What happens if I miss a payment?

We'll notify you with a 3-day grace period before any service interruption. We work with our drivers — just reach out and we'll find a solution together.

Do you charge a percentage of my loads?

No — unlike many dispatchers who take a cut of every load, SwiftHaul charges a flat monthly fee. Everything you earn beyond that is 100% yours to keep.

Is the annual plan billed upfront?

Yes, the annual plan is billed once per year and gives you a 20% discount compared to month-to-month billing. It's ideal if you're committed to growing your operation.

Do you offer custom pricing for large fleets?

Absolutely. For fleets of 10 or more trucks, we offer fully custom pricing. Contact us directly and we'll build a package that makes sense for your operation.
