Quick Answers

Common Questions

Quick answers to things people ask us most before reaching out.

How fast will you respond to my message?

We respond to all emails and form submissions within 2 hours during business hours. For urgent matters, calling or WhatsApp messaging is the fastest way to get a response.

Can I contact you anytime, even at night?

Our dispatch support line is available 24/7. You can call or WhatsApp anytime and a real team member will respond. For general inquiries, office hours apply (PKT).

How long does getting a quote take?

After you submit the quote form, a dispatcher reviews your details and calls you back within 2 hours. Most quotes are turned around the same day.

Do I need an appointment to visit the office?

Walk-ins are welcome during office hours in Lahore. However, scheduling a visit in advance ensures a dedicated team member is available to meet with you.

I already registered — how do I reach my dispatcher?

Once onboarded, your dispatcher's direct WhatsApp and phone number is shared with you during the welcome call. You can reach them by call or WhatsApp 24/7.

Do you operate across all of Pakistan?

Yes — we cover all major routes across Pakistan including Punjab, Sindh, KPK, Balochistan, and key intercity corridors like the Karachi–Lahore and Peshawar–Karachi routes.
