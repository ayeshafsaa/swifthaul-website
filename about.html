Our Story

Built by Truckers, for Truckers

SwiftHaul was founded in 2019 by a team of former truck drivers and logistics professionals who understood firsthand the challenges owner-operators face every day. We were tired of watching hard-working drivers lose money to empty miles, bad brokers, and mountains of paperwork.

We started with just 3 dispatchers and 12 drivers in Lahore, Pakistan. Today, we serve over 500 drivers across 4 provinces, handling thousands of loads every month — and we're just getting started.

Our mission has never changed: make dispatching simple, transparent, and profitable for every driver we work with. Whether you're a solo owner-operator or managing a fleet of 20 trucks, we treat your business like our own.
