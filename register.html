What You Need

Registration Requirements

Make sure you have these documents and qualifications ready before registering.

Licensing & Documents

  • Valid Heavy Vehicle Driving License
  • Original CNIC (National Identity Card)
  • Clean driving record
  • Vehicle Registration Documents

Insurance Requirements

  • Valid truck insurance policy
  • Cargo insurance (recommended)
  • Third party liability coverage
  • Route permit where required

Equipment Requirements

  • Truck in good working condition
  • Valid fitness certificate
  • Smart phone for load communication
  • WhatsApp for dispatcher updates

Dispatcher Agreement

By registering with SwiftHaul Dispatch, you agree to the following terms:

1. Services

SwiftHaul will act as your dedicated dispatch representative with freight brokers and shippers. We will source loads, negotiate rates, and manage communication on your behalf.

2. Fees & Billing

Monthly dispatch fees apply as per your selected plan. Fees are billed at the start of each month. SwiftHaul charges a flat monthly fee only — we do not take a percentage of your load earnings.

3. Termination

Either party may terminate this agreement at any time with 7 days written notice. There are no long-term contracts or exit penalties.

4. Liability

SwiftHaul is not liable for load cancellations, delays, or payment disputes caused by third-party brokers or shippers. We will, however, assist in resolving such disputes on your behalf.

5. Driver Responsibilities

You confirm that all provided information is accurate, your vehicle has valid registration and insurance, and you hold a valid heavy vehicle driving license.

6. Amendments

SwiftHaul reserves the right to update these terms. Drivers will be notified of any changes via WhatsApp or email with at least 14 days notice.

Privacy Policy

SwiftHaul Dispatch is committed to protecting your personal information. This policy explains what we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

We collect your name, phone number, email address, CNIC number, driving license number, truck details, and preferred routes when you register or contact us.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely for onboarding, dispatching, load matching, and communicating updates about our services. We do not use your data for unrelated marketing.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share limited information (such as your name and truck type) with verified brokers and shippers strictly for load booking purposes.

4. Data Security

Your data is stored securely and accessible only to authorised SwiftHaul staff. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

5. Your Rights

You may request to view, update, or delete your personal data at any time by contacting us at dispatch@swifthaul.com.

6. Consent

By submitting the registration form, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this policy.
